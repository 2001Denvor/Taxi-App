How to Apply Cream

Follow these simple steps for best results:

  1. Cleanse: Wash your face or body with a mild cleanser and pat dry.
  2. Take a small amount: Use your fingertips to take a small amount of cream.
  3. Apply gently: Dot the cream onto your skin and massage in circular motion.
  4. Don’t overuse: A little goes a long way! Apply evenly without excess.
  5. Use daily: Apply once or twice a day, preferably morning and night.

Tip: For best results, follow up with sunscreen during the day.
